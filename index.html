This app creates an overlay to your lockscreen which keeps the screen awake. To save battery, please disable auto brightness and set screen timeout to lowest. Don't use display mode which uses sensors such as lift to show or wave to show. It is recommended to use notification show or show while charging in order to save battery.

As per the android system, When a service runs in foreground they must show a persistent notification. This app is required to run in foreground to avoid force closed by system. So that you can have an uninterrupted and seamless experience. To disable this notification, long press on the notification and in the opened settings, turn off Allow notifications and turn back it on. It will hide the notification until you restart your device.

To save device battery, the screen timeout option is set to a default value of 15 seconds. To change it goto Display settings of the app and find the screen timeout option then change it as per your choice. If you don't want the Always On Screen to be turn off, You can set the timeout to never. However, it is not recommended at all. Doing this may cause battery drain faster and also can pixels of your screen if it is Amoled type. Do this at your own risk. I'm not responsible if any damage causes to your device.

Make sure at least one of the widgets is turned on. Turn your screen off and wait for the Always On Screen to show. Now double tap on the clock and then swipe left or right for the widgets. You can't swipe the clock when it is dim. All the touch inputs are disabled to prevent accidental touches. You can enable the interaction whenever you want by just double tapping on the clock. It will automatically disable after 5 seconds of inactivity.

Go to Miscellaneous option the Always On Screen settings. Enable the fingerprint option. You can change the type of fingerprint scanner you have. If your device has In-display fingerprint scanner, make sure the clock is dim while unlocking.If it's not, wait for few seconds till it dims and then put your finger at the scanner, otherwise the scanner will not work.

Notification LED light doesn't lit up when an app is overlaying your lockscreen and keeping your screen turned on. However you can enable notification option within the app to quickly view your notifications.
